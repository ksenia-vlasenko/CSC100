Best Places To Go

So many hotels to choose from! Make sure to pick the best one for you!

The Hive Hotel

Featuring a shared rooftop terrace, The Hive Hotel is 300 m from Santa Maria Maggiore Basilica. The hotel provides free WiFi throughout and air-conditioned rooms.

The Liberty Boutique Hotel

Offering free WiFi, the Liberty Boutique Hotel features accommodations in Rome. Around 701 m from Sapienza University of Rome, the property is also 901 m away from Santa Maria Maggiore.

Tree Charme Spagna Boutique Hotel

Offering 2 terraces, Tree Charme Spagna Boutique Hotel is set in the historical center of Rome, 350 m from the Spanish Steps. It features elegant accommodations with free WiFi.
